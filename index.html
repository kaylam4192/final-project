Why should i stop using makeup that has been tested on animals?

The makeup industry is a billion dollar industry, making most of their money by selling rich colors, products that make your eyes appear larger, and confidence to millions across the world! However, these billion dollar companies often use animals to test the makeup we use everyday.

But testing vegan ingredients on an animal is better, right?

Technically, the word “vegan” is any food, product, clothing, etc. that does not contain any ingredients that came from an animal. Many think that “vegan” is another word for “all-natural” but that is definitely not the case. In other words, vegan ingredients should still not be tested on animals, because they may still contain harsh chemicals that can hurt our precious animals!
